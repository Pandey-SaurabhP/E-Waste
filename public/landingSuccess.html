About our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to make responsible choices when disposing of e-waste by providing easy access to the nearest collection and recycling facilities. We aim to educate users on the environmental and health impacts of improper disposal and incentivize sustainable practices by rewarding them with credits for recycling precious metals from their old devices. Together, we strive for a greener, cleaner future.

About this Program

Our program is a user-friendly website that locates the nearest e-waste collection and recycling facilities. It educates users about the harmful consequences of improper e-waste disposal. Users can also input their old device model to earn credits based on precious metals recovery. Promoting responsible e-waste management and environmental sustainability is our core mission.

Why proper E-waste disposal matters?

Electronic waste (e-waste) contains harmful substances that can pollute the environment and pose health risks when not disposed of correctly. Responsible disposal is essential for safeguarding our planet and well-being.

Challenges and Realities of E-Waste Management in India

India faces a growing e-waste challenge, with approximately 3 million metric tons generated annually, making it one of the world's largest producers. However, only about 5% of this e-waste is formally recycled due to limited awareness and infrastructure. This issue poses environmental and health risks, as e-waste often contains hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium. Informal recycling methods, such as open burning and acid baths, are prevalent, further exacerbating these concerns. Addressing this issue is crucial, not only to mitigate environmental degradation but also to tap into the economic potential of recycling and sustainable e-waste management, creating green jobs and reducing resource depletion.
